The Problem

Nobody sees the full picture. Not even you.

Your wealth is spread across multiple advisors, custodians, entities, and asset classes. You track it in a spreadsheet, a folder of PDFs, or your head. No single tool handles the PE fund and the rental properties and the operating business and the trusts all in one view.

How It Works

Three steps to a complete picture.

01

Add your assets.

Every asset class: liquid investments, real estate, private equity, operating businesses, personal property, royalties. Add as much or as little detail as you want. Our onboarding team helps with the heavy lifting.

02

Organize your structure.

Document your entities (trusts, LLCs, LPs, foundations) and which assets they hold. See the full ownership picture in one view.

03

Control access.

Invite a spouse, business partner, advisor, or family member. Set permissions for each person. You decide who sees what, and you can change it anytime.

Beyond Organization

Document your playbook. Share it on your terms.

Right now, you know where everything is. You know who manages what, which entities hold which assets, and where the documents are. That knowledge has real value: to your family, your partners, and your advisors.
